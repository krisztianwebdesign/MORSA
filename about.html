About Morsa

Born in Leith. Baked with care.

From Dough to Dream

Morsa was born in a Leith kitchen with little more than flour, passion, and a moka pot. What began as an experiment soon became a neighbourhood staple.

The goal was simple — to create a space where people felt welcome and left with a full heart (and belly). What followed was a journey of taste, community, and heritage.

Local Love

From simple beginnings, Morsa grew into a beloved café — rooted in warmth, flavour, and soul.

Our focus is on quality and connection. Whether it’s a rosemary-topped focaccia or a strong flat white, we put our heart into every detail.

A Bite of Morsa

Every bite tells a story. We make bread, yes — but also memories, laughter, and comfort.

It’s more than focaccia. It’s a feeling that lingers long after your last sip or crumb.

Meet the Founders

Morsa didn’t begin in a boardroom — it began with three friends arguing over how crispy focaccia should be and who makes the stronger moka coffee.

What started as weekend baking experiments in a Leith kitchen turned into a full-blown flavour obsession. Flour was everywhere. Ideas bounced. Burnt batches happened. But the community grew — and so did the dream.

— The Morsa Founders
