About Morsa

At Morsa, we slow bake every loaf and pour every shot with intention. From our hand-stretched focaccia infused with bold flavours to our locally roasted espresso, everything we serve is rooted in comfort, craft, and calm.

Read More

Crafted for Comfort

Our dough rises slow and bakes golden in the morning light. We pair hand-made focaccia with bold espresso to bring you the warmth of a southern Italian bakery — right here in Leith.
